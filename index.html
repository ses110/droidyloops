Droidyloops

A portable music sequencer for Android

Project maintained by sid9102 and ses110

Introducing: DroidyLoops

Make loops and put them together as songs, on Android!

Features

A fully dynamic and fluid UI.

Supports a virtually infinite number of customisable tracks.

Make loops on the fly!

Record your voice or environmental sounds, and add it to your song.

Play back your creations!

Description

A quick and easy to use loop editor for fast song-making on the go. The main philosophy behind the creation of DroidyLoops was to make playing around with loops and making simple songs as easy as possible. Record your own samples and make songs entirely out of your voice, or bang some pots and pans together to add an interesting texture to your music!
